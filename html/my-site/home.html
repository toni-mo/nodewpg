Use your Notebook

  • Create a plan, list of tasks for todays work
  • Draw a sketch
  • Note your progress
  • Note problems you encounter
  • Note how you solved them
  • Spill out your thoughts on the paper
  • You can use your notebook everywhere

Think Abstractly

  • Transition complex ideas to simplier ones
  • Simplify things
  • Use universal programming language. It exceeds boundaries of all programming language
  • Just like this guy to the right
  • Transition to your field of interest. It can be sport, hobby, etc.

Look at the problem from different angles

  • Try to solve problem as it is
  • Look from a slightly different angle
  • Look from another angle
  • Look in a totaly different place that has no connection to this problem. Sometimes you can find solution in unexpected places

Give your brain a rest

  • Leave your task for a while
  • Distract yourself from your work
  • Do some workout
  • Take a good walk on fresh air
  • Working on computer your brain gets poluted. It needs time to refresh itself
  • Fresh mind takes on tasks much more efficient

Work Incrementaly

  • Select one small feature you will be working on
  • Create a list of tasks for this feature
  • Check what is finnished and what is yet to be done
  • Try to make deadlines for each feature
  • Add up one feature(part of code) each time
